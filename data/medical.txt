MEDICAL INFORMATION FILE
------------------------
- Fasting blood sugar normal range: 70 to 99 mg/dL.
- Normal blood pressure is usually around 120/80 mmHg.
- A healthy BMI for most adults is between 18.5 and 24.9.
- Staying hydrated requires drinking at least 8 glasses of water a day.
- Regular exercise for at least 30 minutes a day can improve heart health.
